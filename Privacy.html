Privacy Policy for AI Card Cutter

Effective Date: September 2025

1. Introduction

AI Card Cutter is a Chrome extension that helps debaters format evidence into judge-ready debate cards. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

2. Data Collection

This extension processes only the text that you highlight on webpages to generate debate cards. The extension does not collect or transmit personally identifiable information, browsing history, or other sensitive data.

3. Data Usage

4. Data Sharing

AI Card Cutter does not share your data with any external parties. All processing occurs locally in the browser.

5. Permissions

The extension requests limited Chrome permissions (activeTab, contextMenus, storage, scripting, and downloads) strictly to enable its single purpose: cutting and exporting debate cards. No extra permissions are requested or used.
